Marjal Costa Blanca Camping & Resort

In a palatial high-rise building, this lavish hotel is 12 minutes' walk from the Taipei Fine Arts Museum, 10 km from the Taipei 101 skyscraper and an 8-minute walk from Jian Tan metro station. The refined rooms feature minibars, free Wi-Fi and flat-screens, plus balconies with city views. Suites add separate living rooms. Room service is provided.
